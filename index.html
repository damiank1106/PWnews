Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PH News collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app.

Open Website

1. Overview

PH News is a Philippine news reader app that displays articles from publicly available RSS feeds. The app is designed to provide a fast, modern reading experience on Android phones and tablets.

2. Information We Collect

PH News is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. Depending on how the app is used, it may store limited app-related data locally on your device, such as:

  • theme preference such as light mode or dark mode
  • saved bookmarks or favorite articles
  • cached news articles for faster loading and offline reading
  • basic app settings and sync preferences

3. News Sources

The app reads publicly available RSS feeds and displays article previews, images, titles, descriptions, source names, and publication dates.

When you choose to open a full article, you may be redirected to the original publisher’s website. Those websites may have their own privacy policies and data practices.

4. Local Storage

To improve speed and support offline access, PH News may temporarily store synced articles on your device. Cached news articles are kept for up to 7 days and older items are automatically removed.

5. Synchronization

The app may synchronize RSS content automatically once per day, or when manually refreshed by the user. This synchronization is used only to download recent news content and update the local cache.

6. Personal Data

At this time, PH News does not require account registration and does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as your full name, phone number, home address, or payment information.

7. Data Sharing

PH News does not sell your personal data. The app’s main function is to display content from public RSS feeds. If you open a source article outside the app, the third-party publisher may collect information according to its own policies.

8. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep locally stored app data secure. However, no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

PH News is not specifically directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. External Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites, including original news publishers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of third-party websites.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app improvements, feature changes, or legal updates. Any updated version will be published on this page.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the app developer through the contact method provided in the app store listing or project page.
